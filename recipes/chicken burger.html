Chicken burger

Description

A bun with a cooked chicken breast as filling

    Ingredients :

    1. Ground chicken
    2. Bread crumbs
    3. Onion
    4. Egg
    5. Seasonings
    6. Oil
    7. Bun

Step-by-step on how to make chicken burger

  1. Mix ground chicken, 1/4 cup bread crumbs, onion, egg, and garlic in a bowl. Season with salt and pepper
  2. Moisten hands and shape chicken mixture, 2 tablespoon at a time, into flat, oval-shaped patties
  3. Spread remaining 1/4 cup bread crumbs in a shallow dish. Dip patties in bread to crumbs to coat
  4. Heat olive oil in a large
  5. Cook patties in hot oil until deeply browned on the bottoms, 5 to 6 minutes. Turn patties and cook until other sides browned, 3 to 4 minutes more.
  6. Serve with patties bun
